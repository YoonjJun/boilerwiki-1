Apply for a Social Security Number

If you work at Purdue and receive a paycheck, you may be eligible to get a Social Security Number (SSN). An SSN is a number that serves many purposes, like a social security number in South Korea, and is required to receive paychecks and pay taxes. To apply for an SSN, you'll need to visit the Social Security Administration Office in Lafayette. You'll need the following documents to apply. We have instructions in English on how to apply.

  • Passport + visa
  • Entry document (I-94)
  • DS-2019 for J-1
  • I-20 for F-1
  • A sponsor letter from your school or evidence of on-campus employment
  • Application (SSA-4)

After you apply, you will be mailed a Social Security Number Card with your SSN on it. This card is an important record and you should keep it so you don't lose it.
